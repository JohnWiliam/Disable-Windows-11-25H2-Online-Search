## 2024-05-23 - Contextual Tooltips for Action Buttons
**Learning:** Users often hesitate to click "Apply" or "Revert" without knowing the exact consequences or scope of the action.
**Action:** Always provide `ToolTip`s on action buttons that explain *what* will happen (e.g., "Restores default Windows behavior") rather than just repeating the button label. This reduces cognitive load and improves accessibility.
